## 2. Anti-Ban Strategy for Streaming/Copyright

### Key Principles:

1. **We are NOT a streaming service** — the app is a **synchronization tool**. Users provide their own content via URLs or local files. We do NOT host, cache, or distribute copyrighted media.

2. **No built-in media sources** — The app does NOT include links to pirated content. Users paste their own URLs (YouTube, Vimeo, personal media servers like Plex/Jellyfin, or direct MP4 links).

3. **User-generated content model** — Under App Store Review Guidelines 5.2.1 (User-Generated Content), implement:
   - Report mechanism for inappropriate content
   - Terms of Service prohibiting copyrighted content
   - Ability to block/report rooms

4. **DMCA Compliance** — Publish a DMCA agent contact and takedown procedure at privacy policy URL.

5. **Age Rating: 17+** — Due to uncontrolled user content, rate the app appropriately.

6. **Do NOT mention piracy, torrents, or free movies** anywhere in metadata or screenshots.

7. **Screenshots strategy**:
   - Show ONLY generic/freely available content (Creative Commons videos, user's own photos)
   - Use placeholder content like "Your Movie Night" with generic thumbnails
   - Emphasize the SOCIAL aspect (voice chat, friends list, sync indicators)

8. **Review Notes** (для reviewer):
```
This app is a social synchronization tool. It does NOT host, stream, or distribute any media content.

All media is user-provided via:
- Direct URLs to legally hosted content
- Personal media servers (Plex, Jellyfin)
- Local file playback

We have:
✅ DMCA takedown procedure at syncwatch.app/dmca
✅ Content reporting in every room
✅ Terms of Service prohibiting copyrighted content sharing
✅ No built-in media libraries or sources
```
